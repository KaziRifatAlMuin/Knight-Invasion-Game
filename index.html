Rules & Mechanics

Objective

Player 1 (Blue) starts at row 0 and must reach row 8. Player 2 (Red) starts at row 8 and must reach row 0. First to reach the opponent's starting row wins.

Knight Movement

  • Moves in an L-shape: 2 squares one way, 1 square perpendicular
  • Cannot land on fire cells, blocks, or the opponent
  • Must stay within the 9×9 board boundaries

Blocking

  • Place exactly 2 permanent blocks on empty cells instead of moving
  • Blocks cannot be placed on fire, other blocks, or any knight
  • Both players MUST retain a valid path to their goal row after any block placement — enforced by BFS
  • If no valid pair of blocks exists, you must move

Fire Hazard

🔥 Fire cells are generated symmetrically at game start. No knight can move onto fire. If your knight is adjacent (including diagonally) to any fire cell, you must move that turn — blocking is disabled.

Difficulty

  • Easy: 4–8 fire cells on the board
  • Medium: 8–12 fire cells on the board
  • Hard: 12–16 fire cells on the board

Turn Order

  • Players alternate turns — one action per turn
  • Either move the knight OR place two blocks, never both
  • No passing — you must take an action each turn
